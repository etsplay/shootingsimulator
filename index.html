Rifle-gamepad: shooting training simulator

Purpose of the Simulator

Virtual shooting simulator provides a realistic training environment tailored to the physical and technical demands of competition.

The simulator allows you to acquire:

  • Skills in using an air and small-bore rifle.
  • Understanding the importance of ergonomic settings during long-term shooting under stress.
  • Getting used to the sensitivity of the trigger.
  • Training muscle memory when searching for a balance of muscle tension.

    • Realistic Air Rifle Simulation

    The simulator is based on an air rifle model designed to simulate the use of professional, competition-level rifles. The digital interface accurately tracks aiming and movement, providing instant feedback without the need for live ammunition or shooting ranges.

    Ergonomic Customization

    The simulator allows you to physically adjust the layout of the rifle elements and the grip point to the user's body dimensions. This ensures proper shooting posture and builds essential muscle memory.

    • Portable and Safe

    Unlike traditional shooting setups, this simulator is completely portable and safe for indoor use, even in schools or gyms. No need for lead bullets, rests or ear protection. Ideal for athletes with disabilities.
